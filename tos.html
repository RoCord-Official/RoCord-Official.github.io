RoCord ToS

1. Strong Language

Using strong language is against the Roblox ToS so it's also prohibited in RoCord, if you get caught by the RoCord Warning System you will gain a warning/strike.

2. Inappropriate Images

Sending inappropriate images is against the Roblox ToS so it's also prohibited in RoCord, if you get caught by the RoCord Warning System you will gain a warning/strike.

Warning/Strike System explained below.

3. Warning System

With the Warning System, each user gets three warnings/strikes.
One warning/strike is 5 minutes timeout from sending messages and images.
Two warnings/strikes is 15 minutes timeout from sending and receiving messages and images.
Three and Final warnings/strikes is your RoCord token getting removed and your global username banned from creating another token.
