RoCord Privacy Policy

Data Collection

1. RoCord collects the users global name to store in the RoCord token database.

2. RoCord collects the timestamp of when the interaction was made in the RoCord token database.

Why do we collect it?

We collect the users global name to match the RoCord token with an account and we collect the timestamp to check if its a real RoCord token.

Data Removal

If you want to remove your global name in the RoCord database you can do so by running /remove in the Discord Server.

By removing your global name you remove your RoCord token as well.

How to Contact

Any problems can be posted in the #issues-bugs channel in the Discord Server or can be sent to the RoCord email.

Disclamer: the RoCord email isn't as active as the Discord Server.
